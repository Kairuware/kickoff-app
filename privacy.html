Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

✅ KickOff does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works completely offline and requires no account or sign-up.

1. Information We Collect

KickOff does not collect any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any other personal details.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

KickOff stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • World Cup 2026 fixture data (match times, venues, teams)
  • Your alarm preferences (which matches you set alarms for)
  • App settings (default alarm time, last sync time)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server or third party.

3. Permissions Used

KickOff requests the following Android permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to send you match alarm notifications
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — to fire alarms at the precise kickoff time
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to restore scheduled alarms after device restart
  • INTERNET — to optionally sync fixture updates (no personal data sent)
  • VIBRATE — to vibrate your device when an alarm fires

4. Internet Access

KickOff may use internet access to sync fixture schedule updates (such as rescheduled matches) from a sports data API. This sync only requests publicly available fixture data and does not send any personal information or device identifiers.

5. Third Party Services

KickOff does not use any analytics services, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or social media integrations. No third party has access to your data.

6. Children's Privacy

KickOff does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
